Our Services

Microblading

Microblading is creating 3D hair like strokes using microfine needles. This technique is suitable for who have plenty of existing hair to blend with the created strokes. Great for those who have good amount of hair and want more volume. For those who have little to no hair, it’s not recommended as the strokes will not blend naturally. Suitable for dry or normal skin. If you have oily skin, please opt for Ombré Brows. Lasts upto 24 Months.

Ksh 20,000

Combo Brows

This technique is a combination of full microblading with a little bit of shading for more defined but still on the natural side, suitable for who have plenty of existing hair to blend with the created strokes. Great for those who have good amount of hair and want more volume. For those who have little to no hair, it’s not recommended as the strokes will not blend naturally. Suitable for dry or normal skin type. Lasts upto 2 years.

Ksh 30,000

Ombre Brows

The Ombre Brows is a semi-permanent technique for clients who prefer a more natural, longer lasting result. It is among the newest trends in eyebrow semi-permanent makeup. It gives a soft shaded brow pencil look. Once healed, it is more natural looking than solid color.Whether you are looking for a light soft shading or show-stopping bold brows, this technique can be customized according to your desire. Lasts upto 3 years.

Ksh 25,000

Before Treatment

  • DO NOT take Fish Oil, Aspirin, Niacin, vitamin E, and/or Ibuprofen unless prescribed by a doctor and absolutely necessary 72 hours before the procedure.
  • DO NOT work out 24 hrs prior to the procedure.
  • DO NOT use AHA skincare products close to the enhancement area 2 weeks prior to and after your procedure.
  • DO NOT consume any alcohol or alcoholic beverages 24 HOURS before the scheduled appointment. This helps in preventing any excess bleeding and minimizing swelling during the procedure.
  • DO NOT wax, thread or tint your eyebrows one week before your procedure.
  • Avoid facials or chemical peels a week prior to the scheduled appointment.
  • Avoid sun and tanning as much as possible one week prior to your procedure.
  • Retin-A, Glycolics and Retinol products should be discontinued 4 weeks before the scheduled appointment.
  • If there are any open wounds, pimples, bruises, or any other skin irritations on the area; please wait for them to be completely healed before your operation. In regard to this, please contact immediately if rescheduling is necessary due to this matter.
  • In the case of exfoliation products at home, please use on the brow area a week prior to the appointment for the skin to take in the pigment(s) easily during the procedure.

After Treatment

  • Immediately after the procedure, brows will appear dark and very defined before the scabbing phase for a couple of days. However, the thickness will subside gradually day by day.
  • There may be whiteness or blanching around the area, which is perfectly normal and will subside within 12 hours. In case you find any lymphatic fluid or blood weeping you can gently clean the area with cool boiled water and gauze, blotting gently dry to remove any moisture.
  • Redness and burning sensation is normal and should subside by the following day completely. If the area feels unbearable to you, please use a clean ice pack for comfort.
  • You will go through three healing phases: 1-heal, 2-peel and 3-fade.
  • Once the brow area starts to peel, it will appear flaky and unappealing. The area may feel a bit dry. Do not pick or pull at the treated area as it will result in pigment loss
  • Once all scabs have fully fallen off completely and naturally, healed results should appear 20-30% lighter in color and about 15% smaller in size from the initial. Kindly consider using a waterproof total sunblock when going out in the sun to stop the color fading.

Kindly note

Permanent cosmetic enhancements normally require multiple application sessions. To achieve the best results, you will be required to return for at least one control procedure after the initial application. Be prepared for the color intensity to be significantly sharper and darker immediately after the procedure. This will subside and become softer as the skin heals. This process can take up to 14 days.
