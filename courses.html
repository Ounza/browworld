Our Classes

We teach you how to become a microblading expert through rigorous classes with our flexible training options to interested individuals.

You do not need to be a beautician, stylist, or skilled in any of the permanent makeup techniques below to join the class.

All you need is a willingness to learn.

Microblading

Journey with us as we show you how to be an absolute master in Microblading. Microblading is creating 3D hair like strokes using microfine needles. This technique is suitable for who have plenty of existing hair to blend with the created strokes. Great for those who have good amount of hair and want more volume. For those who have little to no hair, itâ€™s not recommended as the strokes will not blend naturally. We will take you from a beginner to an expert microblading stylist in this course.

Combo Brows

Here, we take a deep dive into a combination of Microblading and some shading. This technique is a combination of full microblading with a little bit of shading for more defined but still on the natural side, suitable for who have plenty of existing hair to blend with the created strokes. We shall teach you what works and what doesn't while blending the strengths of these two techniques together

Ombre Brows

The Ombre Brows is a semi-permanent technique for clients who prefer a more natural, longer lasting result. It is among the newest trends in eyebrow semi-permanent makeup. It gives a soft shaded brow pencil look. Ombre Brows is more natural looking than solid color and highly in demand in the current market. This course will teach you the tips and tricks in Ombre Brows and make your eyebrows a complete work of art after you become an Ombre Brows stylist.
